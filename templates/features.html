ReadSteed Features

A minimal interface packed with maximal capabilities.

🎯 ORP Targeting

ReadSteed algorithmically calculates the Optimal Recognition Point of every word and highlights it in red, allowing your brain to process it instantly without ocular scanning.

⚡ Precision Speed Control

Adjust your reading speed dynamically with keyboard shortcuts from a casual 100 WPM to an intense 1000 WPM. The app dynamically slows down for long complex words and punctuation.

🧘 Focus Mode

Eliminate all distractions. Focus mode hides UI menus, buttons, and sidebars, transforming your device screen into a dedicated, pure black canvas for the words.

☁️ Cloud Library

Create a free account to automatically save your reading progress. Stop midway through a long essay on your laptop, and resume exactly where you left off on your phone.

🎨 Complete Theming

Your reading environment should map to your preferences. Select from custom fonts (like high-legibility Inter), and pre-built Dark, Light, or Sepia themes.

📊 Live Analytics

Before you start, ReadSteed calculates the exact time commitment required based on your current WPM setting. Always know how long an article will take.
